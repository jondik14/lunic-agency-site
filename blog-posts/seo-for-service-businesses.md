For service-based businesses, local SEO is often the difference between thriving and struggling. Here's how to dominate local search.

## Why Local SEO Matters

When someone searches for "plumber near me" or "web designer Sydney," they're ready to buy. Local SEO helps you appear in these critical searches.

## Google Business Profile Optimization

### Complete Your Profile

- Accurate business information (name, address, phone)
- Business hours (including special hours)
- Business category selection
- Photos of your work and team
- Services list
- Regular posts and updates

### Get Reviews

- Ask satisfied clients for reviews
- Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
- Make it easy for clients to leave reviews
- Include review link in email signatures

## On-Page Local SEO

### Location Pages

If you serve multiple areas, create location-specific pages:
- "Web Design Sydney"
- "Web Design Melbourne"
- Each with unique, valuable content

### Local Keywords

Naturally include location-based keywords:
- In page titles
- In headings
- In content
- In meta descriptions

### NAP Consistency

Ensure your Name, Address, Phone (NAP) is consistent:
- On your website
- In Google Business Profile
- In directory listings
- Across all platforms

## Local Content Strategy

### Service Area Pages

Create dedicated pages for each service area:
- "Website Design for Sydney Businesses"
- "Conversion Optimization for Service Companies"

### Local Blog Content

Write about local topics:
- "Best Practices for Sydney Service Businesses"
- "Local Market Trends"
- Case studies with local businesses

### Local Citations

Get listed in local directories:
- Industry-specific directories
- Local business directories
- Chamber of commerce
- Local news sites

## Technical SEO for Local

### Schema Markup

Add LocalBusiness schema to your site:
```json
{
  "@type": "LocalBusiness",
  "name": "Your Business Name",
  "address": {
    "@type": "PostalAddress",
    "addressLocality": "Sydney",
    "addressRegion": "NSW"
  }
}
```

### Mobile Optimization

Local searches happen on mobile. Ensure your site:
- Loads quickly on mobile
- Is easy to navigate
- Has click-to-call buttons
- Shows directions easily

## Link Building for Local SEO

### Local Partnerships

- Partner with complementary businesses
- Sponsor local events
- Join local business associations
- Get featured in local news

### Local Directories

Submit to relevant local directories:
- Yelp
- Yellow Pages
- Industry-specific directories
- Local chamber of commerce

## Measuring Local SEO Success

Track these metrics:
- Google Business Profile views
- Direction requests
- Phone calls from website
- Local search rankings
- "Near me" search traffic

## Quick Wins

1. **Claim and optimize Google Business Profile** - Free and highly effective
2. **Get 5+ reviews** - Builds trust and improves rankings
3. **Add location to every page** - Make it easy for Google to understand
4. **Create location-specific content** - Show you serve specific areas
5. **Ensure mobile-friendliness** - Most local searches are mobile

Local SEO is an ongoing effort, but the results are worth it. Start with these basics and build from there.
